Seth Rollins Debuts New Ring Gear Tonight (Spoiler Photo), SD! Main Event Changes, Ambrose in Action

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, October 7, 2024

Smackdown Main Event Changes

It was announced on Raw this week that Smackdown would be headlined by Roman Reigns vs Alberto Del Rio, however that has been changed, and two main events were taped tonight for this week's show.

One match saw Dean Ambrose face Kane, and the other is a 4 on 3 Handicap match featuring Roman Reigns, John Cena and Sheamus vs Alberto Del Rio, Randy Orton, Cesaro and Bray Wyatt.
