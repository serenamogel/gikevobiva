Who Is Waffler69 Brother Clayton? Family And Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 4, 2024

Tiktok star Waffler69 died on January 11, 2023; Waffler69 brother has confirmed the news via video. 

TikTok creator Waffler69, the actual name Taylor Brice LeJeune, has died at 33. He died from a presumed heart attack; he was taken to the hospital before death.

Taylor has crossed 1 million followers on his TitTok; people used to love his content. Before being active on TikTok, he used to live stream on Twitch.

He often used to do live on Twitch and post videos in Tiktok as he was banned from TikTok live for seven years. Also, Taylor used to get connected with the audience through Discord.

People were shocked to hear the news from his brother; after he said the information via TikTok video, the video got lots of attention, and condolence has been flooding.

Who Is Waffler69 Brother Clayton?

Clayton is Waffler69’s brother, who shared news about his brother’s death via TikTok video; his Tiktok account goes by claydrom.

The video has gained 1 million views, and people have shared condolence in comments; he has also mentioned it on his Instagram account.

In a video, Clayton said, “This is still pretty new; I don’t know what’s gonna be happening shortly,” Clayton said. “But I thought I should get on TikTok and let everybody know. Please keep his legacy alive.”

Clayton and Talyor share a close relationship, and it is hard for him to believe that his brother is no longer with him.

Clayton has also updated his Instagram bio; his recent one is “Rest in peace, Tylor. Here’s the gofundmelink if you want to donate.” 

To help his mother with funding for cremation, he has shared a link in his bio following the death of Tylor.
