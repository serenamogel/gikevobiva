Sukoon Drama Cast Real Name With Pictures

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, March 18, 2024

Sukoon is a 2023 romantic Pakistani drama serial starring Sana Javed, Khaqan Shahnawaz, and Ahsan Khan in the main cast. The exciting story of drama is written by Misbah Nosheen and directed by Siraj ul Haq. Sana and Ahsan have a huge fan following and their fans just loved their on-screen couple in Sukoon. Read more about the Pakistani drama Sukoon cast real name, age, photos, and much more.

Sukoon Drama Cast Real Name

Sana Javed as Aina

Sana Javed played the lead character of Aina in Sukoon opposite Ahsan Khan. She is a well-known Pakistani actress and model. Her age is 30 years. She got married to Cricket Shoaib Malik. She became part of showbiz in 2012. Sana Javed’s recent famous drama was “Kala Doriya” with Usman Khalid Butt.

Ahsan Khan as Hamdan

Ahsan Khan is known for his brilliant acting and hosting skills. In Sukoon his character name is Hamdan. His age is 42 years old. He started his acting career in 1998. His latest hit serial is Mujhe Qabool Nahi with Madiha Imam. Ahsan Khan’s previous hit serial was “Meray Humnasheen” with Hiba Ali.

Memoona Qudoos as Natasha

Memoona Qudoos has played the role of Natasha in the drama serial Sukoon. Natasha is Raza’s fiance in the drama. Memoona is an emerging Pakistani actress and model who appeared in supporting roles in many dramas. Khumar is her recent drama series.

Khaqan Shahnawaz as Raza

Pakistani model and actor Khaqan Shahnawaz played the role of Raza in the drama Sukoon. Raza is engaged to Natasha. His recent famous dramas were Hadsa and College Gate in 2023. He started his career by modeling and appearing in many photoshoots for top fashion brands in Pakistan.

Sidra Niazi as Shanzay aka Guria

Sidra Niazi has played the role of Shanzay aka Guria in the drama serial Sukoon. Her debut drama was Chupke Cupke with Usman Khalid Butt and Ayeza Khan. She appeared in many hit serials in supporting roles. Her age is 33 years and she is single. Her new drama is “Khushbo Mein Basay Khat”.

Qudsia Ali as Aima

Qudsai Ali performed the role of Aima, the younger sister of Sana Javed. She appeared in many dramas in supporting roles. Her recent hit dramas are Fitna and Batiyaan with Fatima Effendi. She is 25 years old and she lives in Karachi with her family. Kuch Ankahi is the recent drama series of Qudsia Ali in which she played the role of Sajal Aly’s younger sister.

Adnan Samad Khan as Usman

Adnan Samad Khan played the supporting role of Usman in Sukoon. He came into the limelight after performing the character of Gulzar in the super hit Pakistani drama “Ahad e Wafa” with Ahad Raza Mir. His recent drama is “Khumar” with Neelam Munner and Feroze Khan.

Usman Peerzada as Shanzay’s Father

Usman Peerzada is a senior Pakistani drama actor. He played the character of the father of the lead cast of Urdu dramas. In Sukoon he performed the role of father of Sidra Niazi. His recent drama is Mein in which he played the role of the father of Ayeza Khan. His age is 72 years old.

Asma Abbas as Usman’s Mother

Asma Abbas is a well-known Pakistani drama actress. She is famous for performing the role of mother of the lead cast in Pakistani dramas. Amsa Abbas has played the role of Usman’s mother in drama Sukoon. She is 65 years old. Her husband’s name is Abbas Gill. She is the mother of actress Zara Noor Abbas.

Laila Wasti as Aina’s Mother

Laila Wasti is a famous Pakistani drama actress, she played the role of the mother of lead actress Aina. She is 46 years old. Her husband’s name is Fahad Rehmani. She is the daughter of famous actress Tahira Wasti. Her previous hit drama was “Kaisi Teri Khudgarzi”, in which she played the role of the mother of lead actress Dur e Fishan Saleem.

Aehsun Talish as Aina’s Father

Director and Actor Aehsun Talish has performed the role of Aina’s father in the drama serial Sukoon. She is one of the most famous Pakistani drama directors.
