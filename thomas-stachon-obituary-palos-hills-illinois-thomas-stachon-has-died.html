Thomas Stachon Obituary Palos Hills Illinois, Thomas Stachon Has Died

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 14, 2024

Thomas Stachon Obituary, Death Cause – We regret to inform you of the death of 7-year-old Thomas J. Stachon. Surrounded by relatives, Thomas died away unexpectedly on December 8, 2023. He is Richard and Gosia Stachon’s Beloved Son. Dear Brother to Matthew and Anthony. Cherished Grandson of Bronislaw and Zofia Topor-Miesiar, as well as Stanley and Eva Stachon. Dearly adored nephew of Marek Stachon (who passed away), Stephen Stachon, Danuta and Pawel Kalata, Krzysiek and Kasia Topor.

Leonia Wesolowska’s great-grandson. cherished relative by many. Thomas had amazing professors and a large circle of friends. Thomas was a youngster of great kindness, compassion, and care. Thomas’s fondness for playing football, constructing with Legos and roughhousing with his brothers was evident to all who met him. He enjoyed colouring and drawing. He had pleasure in education. Anywhere he went, Thomas possessed a spark that could light up a whole room because he was constantly full of life.

Although Thomas had mentors in his life, many saw him as a greater role model. He had the purest, most compassionate heart, and a grin that brightened the entire space. Always willing to assist in any manner. Whether it was organising a space, soothing his little brother, or giving instructions to others, he was always obliged to make sure he could assist. Thomas’s brief seven years on earth with us have left a lasting impact on our lives, and we shall miss him every day.

His legacy will endure eternally. On Thursday, December 14, 2023, from 4 to 8 PM, a visitation will be place at Skaja Terrace Funeral Home in Niles, Illinois. On Friday, December 15, 2023, at 10 AM, there will be a funeral and mass at 11 AM at St. Rosalie’s Church in Harwood Heights. Maryhill Cemetery is the place of interment.
