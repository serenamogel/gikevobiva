Keith Urban Biography, Wife (Nicole Kidman), Net, Kids, Age &amp; Height

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, April 5, 2024

On any real list of the best country singers in the world, you’ll definitely find Keith Urban, unless the list isn’t long enough. Known for his consistently loud and powerful lyrics, Urban is a singer, songwriter, and record producer who began his career in 1990. Nearly three decades later, he’s already had 11 studio albums selling millions of his records worldwide. around the world and has won numerous awards including Grammys. and Academy of Country Music Awards. He is married to American actress Nicole Kidman.

Keith Urban Bio

October 26, 1967 is when Urban was born Keith Lionel Urban in his home country of New Zealand, Whangarei, Northland. He was raised in Australia alongside his siblings as the youngest child by his parents; Marienne and Robert “Bob” Urban.

Growing up, Keith had always loved music, and so when he was six, his father helped him find a guitar teacher. Thanks to this, he was able to start participating in music competitions, some of which he won. While doing so, he was also learning to act as a member of a local theater company, the Westfield Super Juniors.

By the time he was 16 in 1983, Keith, whose ambition was to become a radio presenter, appeared on the TV show ‘New Faces’ where he was advised to find a teacher for his voice as he had the world behind him.

Only 7 years later, the singer released his self-titled debut album, and in 1992 he moved to where most people who wanted to take country music seriously – Nashville, Tennessee. Although in the years to come he released more singles, his next album, The Ranch came in 1997, as a member of the band, The Ranch. His second album, Keith Urban was released in 1999. Along with many other albums, his most recent works are Ripcord in 2016 and Graffiti U in 2018.

In what can only be described as a very successful career, Keith has released 11 Studio albums including Golden Road which has sold over 3 million copies and Being Here which is a four-time platinum album. More so, he has 4 compilation albums and almost 50 singles.

Despite his best efforts and talent, the Fighter singer has been recognized with numerous awards including multiple Grammy Awards, Academy of Country Music Awards, Country Music Association Awards and Country Music Association Awards Entertainer of the Year which he won in 2018. Ahead of the 61st Annual Grammy Awards, the country singer was nominated for Best Country Solo Performance for his Parallel Line

Wife – Nicole Kidman and Children

It’s hard not to agree that one of the hottest celebrity couples around right now are Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban. For the two, however, it was not love at first sight when they first met in 2005. The next time they met a few months later on their first date.

Six months after their date, Nicole was woken around 5 a.m. by Urban standing at her window with flowers. This early morning, he took her for an intense ride on his bike and that’s when she knew he was the love of her life, her kind of man.

A year later the two got married in Australia and they are now blessed with two children, Sunday Rose Kidman Urban who was born in 2008 and Faith Margaret Kidman Urban born in 2010. According to Nicole who had two children by her previous marriage to Tom Cruise, she would have loved to have 10 children with Keith if they had met when they were 25 years old.

Also Australian like her husband, Nicole Kidman is an actress born in 1967, the same year as Keith. Before meeting Keith, she was first married to American actor Tom Cruise in 1990, but by 2001 the two had separated in a divorce.

Net value

If there’s a problem with Keith Urban, his fortune isn’t one. He has an estimated net worth of $75 million. He has made most of his fortune from his singing career as well as other things he is involved in.

the size

When it comes to his height, Keith Urban isn’t the tallest man you’ll find on many stages. He stands at a height of 5ft 8in which puts him a few inches shorter than his wife whose height is 5ft 10.
