Chandler Hallow Biography Timeline

Chandler Hallow is a famous American YouTuber, social media influencer, and member of the YouTube group MrBeast. Born on December 3, 1998, in North Carolina, Hallow’s passion for creating content began at a young age, and he started posting videos on YouTube while still in high school. He gained recognition for his witty humour, pranks, and challenges, which quickly earned him a large following. In this biography timeline, we will take a closer look at the life and career of Chandler Hallow.

  • 1998: Chandler Hallow was born on December 3 in North Carolina, USA.
  • 2012: Hallow creates his first YouTube channel, “chandlerhallow,” and posts videos.
  • 2016: Hallow graduates from high school and starts attending college.
  • 2017: Hallow meets Jimmy Donaldson, aka MrBeast, at a video gaming event and becomes a member of the MrBeast YouTube group.
  • 2018: Hallow quits college to focus on his YouTube career full-time.
  • 2019: Hallow and the MrBeast group launch the “Team Trees” campaign, raising $20 million to plant 20 million trees worldwide.
