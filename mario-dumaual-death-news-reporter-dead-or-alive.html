Is The ABS-CBN Reporter Dead Or Alive?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 19, 2024

The speculation regarding Mario Dumaual death has been debunked, as the revered ABS-CBN journalist is currently fighting for his life in the hospital after a severe heart attack.

ABS-CBN’s renowned entertainment reporter Mario Dumaual is fighting for his life after a sudden heart attack.

While his critical condition has sparked rumors regarding his survival, the Dumaual family remains optimistic.

Amidst the distressing news, they affirm that the veteran journalist is alive. However, the family’s statement reveals a harrowing battle ahead, illuminating the resilience of a man who has graced Philippine television with unwavering dedication.

Mario Dumaual Death News

The rumors surrounding veteran ABS-CBN reporter Mario Dumaual’s health status have sparked significant concern among fans, friends, and family across the Philippines and beyond.

Many have been alarmed by the sudden hospitalization of the long-standing entertainment journalist after a heart attack.

However, contrary to the rumors of his demise, Dumaual is still in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU).

The disturbing news about his hospitalization was first brought to the public’s attention by his son, Miguel Dumaual, an ABS-CBN reporter.

On his social media, Miguel shared that Mario had a heart attack and was admitted to the ICU.

It has been an emotionally challenging week for the Dumaual family, but they draw strength from the outpouring of support and well-wishes they are receiving.

His son shared, “Papa suffered a heart attack last Monday and remains under critical care today, three days on.

But he can now at least tell us, during the few times he’s given his phone while isolated, that he misses and wishes to be with us.”

Despite his situation, the elder Dumaual’s messages to his family have been a beacon of hope in these trying times.

Friends in the entertainment industry, like Angeli Valenciano, have also rallied their support, offering messages of encouragement.

Valenciano urged her followers to pray for Dumaual, revealing that she had recently urged him to stay strong.

Is The ABS-CBN Reporter Mario Dumaual Dead Or Alive?

Despite the grave situation, the ABS-CBN journalist is not dead but in a critical state at the hospital.

The misinformation regarding Mario’s death has been a source of great distress to the family, friends, and thousands of well-wishers closely following the development of his health status.

Given the severity of his condition, Dumaual remains in the ICU under careful monitoring by a team of specialized doctors. The broadcaster’s condition is undeniably grave, but he displays a fighting spirit, as indicated by his son’s update.

Mario’s messages to his family, expressing his longing to be with them, testify to his resilience and determination to overcome his health crisis.

Despite never being confined to a hospital in the past 32 years, a fact shared by his son, Mario’s current health scare has proved to be a trial for him and his family.

Yet, they remain hopeful, continually seeking positive thoughts from everyone.

The family firmly believes that the coming days will bring more reasons to be grateful. As they navigate this difficult time, they are optimistic about his recovery and look forward to reuniting with him.

In conclusion, while it is true that Mario Dumaual’s health condition is severe, it is equally valid that he is alive and fighting. The news about his death is just a rumor and should be disregarded.

His family, friends, and ABS-CBN request that everyone respect their privacy during this challenging time.

Meanwhile, they encourage everyone to continue offering prayers and positive energy toward Mario’s swift and complete recovery.
