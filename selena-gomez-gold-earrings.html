Shop Selena Gomezs Trendy Gold Earrings

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, February 2, 2024

This is our official petition to switch our closet and jewelry box with Selena Gomez’s. The singer has made herself a fashion guru once again as she falls into the colder seasons with stylish new fits and accessories (pun intended). Gomez has been a longtime mental health advocate, with her latest action donating 100% of Rare Beauty Sephora sales to her nonprofit, Rare Impact Fund, on World Mental Health Day. The mission of the group is to provide support and resources to those in need of a mental health space.

And, in typical Selena Gomez fashion, she made the contribution announcement in the most humble, sleek, and trendy way possible. In the heartwarming Instagram post, Gomez is sporting a plunging off-white sweater, triangular gold earrings, a messy pulled-back bun, and a full face of Rare Beauty makeup, of course. Lucky for you, it just so happens that you can snag the Mejuri Pyra Large Hoops in gold vermeil that she wore in the video for $148. Silver jewelry lovers should opt for the Pyra Large Hoops in sterling silver instead, with a pretty significant price drop from the gold at $98.

The good news is that you can grab the dupe in silver, too. Don’t know what to pair the jewelry with? Gomez’s staple baggy jeans or her nostalgic long denim skirt and your favorite bodysuit would be a perfect match!
