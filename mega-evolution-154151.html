Mega Evolution | Page 99

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 25, 2024
Up until GenV, Arceus is definitely the strongest in terms of BST. And because it is the almight alpha god of Pokemon World, IMO I always thought no Pokemon would surpass it in terms of BST.

But when Mega Evolution introduced in GenVI, such notion had broken to fine dust. Then there is one thing I do not understand. If GF was tended to made Arceus which based off the concept of almighty god, why made its BST of only 720? Excluding Mega Evolutions, it surely is strongest, but still even me view it as the Alpha One, its BST is low. Now when including Mega Evolution, Arceus is downgraded to a second-class deity that is beatable by some ordinary power-up Pokemon.

Curse you GF, insulting my favourite Legendary Pokemon of all time by introducing Mega Evolution with broken BST. I won't forgive it until GF gives Arceus a Mega Evolution with BST of 9999.
