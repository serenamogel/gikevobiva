Addison Timlin Wiki Biography

Born Addison Jayne Timlin on the 29th June 1991 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania USA, she is an actress probably best known for starring in the role of Amy Schine in the film “Derailed” (2005), then playing Sasha Bingham in the Showtime’s series “Californication” (2011), and as Jami in the film “The Town That Dreaded Sundown” (2014). Her career has been active since 2005.

Have you ever wondered how rich Addison Timlin is? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that the total size of Addison’s net worth is over $400,000, as of late 2017, accumulated through her successful involvement in the entertainment industry.

Addison Timlin Net Worth $400,000

Addison Timlin was raised with three siblings by her father, R.J. Timlin, and her mother, Jayne Timlin. From the time she was little, Addison became interested in acting and soon her career began, as she made her debut when she was 14 years old, as a girl with diabetes – Amy – in the film “Derailed”, next to Clive Owen and Melissa George. She then appeared in the short film “The Isabel Fish” in 2006, while in 2008 she got her first recurring role as Emily Draper in the TV comedy-drama series “Cashmere Mafia”, and the same year portrayed Amy in the mystery drama film “Afterschool”, which also added to her wealth.

Three years later Addison made her breakthrough in the film industry by gaining the role of a movie star, Sasha Bingham in the TV drama series “Californication”, and since then has made several notable appearances in rather successful films and TV series, further increasing her net worth. She played Alex in the thriller film “Stand Up Guys” (2012), starring Cristopher Walken, Al Pacino and Alan Arkin, then played Ramsey in the comedy drama “Best Man Down” (2012), and in 2013 featured in the fantasy horror “Odd Thomas” (2013), starring now deceased Anthon Yelchin, then Ashley Sommers, and Leonor Varela. The same year she also played Rachel Lewis in the TV series “Zero Hour”, while in 2014 she had her first starring role, as Jami in the horror thriller “The Town That Dreaded Sundown”, next to Veronica Cartwright and Anthony Anderson.

Most recently, in 2016 she starred in the comedy drama film “Little Sister”, and the same year continued with lead roles in such films as “Girl in the Box” and “Fallen”, and in 2017 “Like Me”. She is currently working on several films, most notably “When I’m a Moth”, a biopic about Hillary Clinton, which will come out in 2018; she will portray young Hillary Clinton.

Regarding Addison Timlin’s personal life, she was dating the actor Connor Paolo in 2005 as well as actor Justin Chatwin for a short period in 2010. At the present time, she is in a relationship with musician Zach Shields. Her current residence is in New York City. In her spare time, she is active on her official Twitter and Instagram profiles. She is a huge fan of tattoos and has more than 30 around her body.
