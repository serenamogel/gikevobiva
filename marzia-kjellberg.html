PewDiePie's Wife Marzia Kjellberg - Pregnant, Wedding, Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 22, 2024

Marzia Kjellberg is one of the known celebrity personalities in this digital world. She is a famous Italian Internet personality, businesswoman, and former Youtuber. 

Marzia is also known as the wife of popular YouTuber PewDiePie. Her husband's real name is Felix Kjellberg known for gaming and engaging content on his youtube channel. 

The First Meet Of Marzia With Felix A.K.A PewDiePie

Marzia's friend Daizo was the one who introduced Marzia to PewDiePie. She suggested PewDiePie's videos to Marzia and she ended up liking them. The two began their conversation and eventually, Marzia and her husband Felix met each other in 2011. 

Marzia and Felix started their long-distance relationship. At the start of their relationship, she used to live in Italy while her lover Felix used to live in Sweden.

Later, love prevailed and Marzia moved from Italy to Sweden to live with Felix in October 2011. Similarly, they moved to Italy but later decided to settle down in Brighton, England in 2013. 

PewDiePie And Marzia's Wedding

Marzia Kjellberg shares a blissful married life with her husband Felix Kjellberg. They are together for almost a decade and have shared married life for over three years. Marzia and Felix announced their marriage via their social media.

Felix made an engagement proposal to Marzia on April 27, 2018, which she posted on her Instagram. A year later, Kjelberg got married to her boyfriend of eight years, Felix in a private wedding ceremony. 

The loving duo Felix and Marzia tied the wedding knot on August 20, 2019, i.e. the anniversary of their first meeting. It was a small and private ceremony completed at London's Kew botanical garden without throwing a big party. 

In the wedding ceremony, the groom Felix wore a classic black tuxedo whereas his bride was wearing a serene blush couture gown.
