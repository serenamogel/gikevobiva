Alessandra Ambrosio aka Alessandra Corine Ambrosio is a Brazil and American model and actress. As of 2023, Alessandra Ambrosio’s net worth is $80 million. She is also an entrepreneur, businesswoman, and fashion designer.

She rose to her prominence in her career after Victoria’s Secret appointed her as a model in 2004. She has been in a contract with Victoria’s Secret for about 17 years. Ambrosio was listed by People magazine in their 2007 “100 Most Beautiful People” in the world list. 

Ambrosio in her dazzling modeling career has worked with many big fashion houses like Ralph Lauren, Gucci, Calvin Klein, Christian Dior, Dolce & Gabbana, and many more.

She has appeared in many popular films like Casino Royale, Daddy’s Home and in its sequel Daddy’s Home 2, Double Dutchess, etc. Ambrosio has been featured in many reality TV shows such as The Tyra Banks Show, The Late Late Show Project Runaway, etc. 

Alessandra Ambrosio Wiki/Biography

Born on 11 April, 1981, Alessandra Ambrosio’s age is 42 Years Old as of 2023. Her birthplace is Erechim, in the Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Ambrosio has citizenship in both Brazil and America. She grew up in Erechim and spent her childhood there. She is a Christian by religion.

Alessandra got her early schooling at a local school in Erechim. She then started getting advanced education at a local high school. Career

Modeling

Alessandra was recognized by Dilson Stein and she started to do modeling under his circle. Ambrosio got a major break in her career when she was offered to be on the cover of Brazilian Elle magazine.

Her recognition by Elle earned her the focus of the Brazilian model industry which further escalated into the international modeling scene. Ambrosio in 2004 was allowed to be the brand ambassador of the fashion company Next which is a UK-based company.

She had also made an appearance in their long-awaited (12 years) tv campaign (commercial). She was given the honorary position of food and cultural correspondent along with model Adriana Lima in the 2016 Rio Summer Olympics.

Ambrosio made a few notable appearances in top fashion editorials along with some high-end celebrities. She had been featured on the cover of GQ magazine (2016 issue) starring alongside an international sensational footballer from Portuguese, Christiano Ronaldo.

She had worked and co-starred with actor Ashton Kutcher to appear on the cover of the Brazilian Sportswear brand, Colcci. Vogue appointed her as their blogger in 2011 (October) and she clicked different photos of her wearing new dresses from her wardrobe and sharing one snap each day which continues 30 days.
