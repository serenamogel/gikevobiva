Sebastian Yatra Biography, Wiki, Age, Height, Family, Career

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Sebastián Obando Giraldo, famous as Sebastian Yatra is a  Singer and songwriter from Colombia. He is well known for reggaeton and hip-pop music. Yatra started learning guitar and piano from childhood. At a very young age, when he attended a concert by the rock band Juanes then he decided to become a musician.
