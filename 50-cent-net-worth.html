50 Cent Biography

50 cent is best known as a rapper. Rap Sensation, who is also a member of the East Coast collective G-Unit and was named the sixth best artist in the 2000s by Billboard Magazine. His first album, “In Da Club,” became a success in 2003. His Get Rich or Die Tryin album was 6x gold, too. He was born on July 6, 1975 in New York City, NY. He was in feuds with Sean Combs, G-Unit, The Game, and Nas. When his mother died, he moved in with his grandparent and his eight aunts and uncles. He competed as a boxer at the Junior Olympics. He 's known to have survived nine gunshot wounds in 2000 and published Guess Who's Back? In the year 2002. The same year, Eminem discovered him and related him to Dr. Dre.
