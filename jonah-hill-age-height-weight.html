Jonah Hill Age, Height, Weight

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Jonah Hill Age, Height, Weight: Jonah Hill, officially known as Jonah Hill Feldstein is an American actor and filmmaker born on December 20, 1983.

He developed a love for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actors.

Jonah Hill is known for his comedic roles in films including Superbad, Knocked Up, 21 Jump Street, This Is the End and 22 Jump Street.

For his performances in Moneyball and The Wolf of Wall Street, he was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor.

As a screenwriter, he contributed to the stories of 21 Jump Street, 22 Jump Street, Sausage Party, Why Him, and You People.

Jonah Hill is also known for his roles in Cyrus, War Dogs, Don’t Worry, He Won’t Get Far On Foot, and Don’t Look Up, to mention a few.

In 2018, he starred in the Netflix miniseries Maniac and made his directorial debut with the film Mid90s, for which he also wrote the screenplay.

Jonah Hill has provided voices for the animated films Horton Hears a Who!, Megamind, How To Train Your Dragon, The Lego Movie, and The Lego Movie 2: The Second Part.
